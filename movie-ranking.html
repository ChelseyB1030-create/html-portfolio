The Best Movies According to Chelsey

My top 3 Favorites!

Nightmare Before Christmas

It is a cult classic that has captured my heart as a child. The Stop animation was incredible for the time it was made, and it still stands on it's own today!

The Neverending Story

Another cult classic, if ever there were any. It is a movie I can watch alone or with my children and enjoy every bit of it!

Repo! The Genetic Opera

If you have not heard of this, and you like rock operas, this movie is for you! There are very talented musicians in it and it is gory and awesome!
